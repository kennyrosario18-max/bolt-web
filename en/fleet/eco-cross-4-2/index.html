Frequently asked questions

What does the rental include?

Delivery and pickup at your villa, charger, usage briefing, vehicle insurance and 24/7 WhatsApp support.

Do I need a license to drive it?

Yes. The driver must be 18 or older with a valid driver's license. Use is allowed only inside residential communities, villas and resorts.

How many people fit in the ECO Cross 4+2?

Up to 6 seated passengers. For safety, standing passengers and exceeding capacity are not allowed.

How does it charge?

It plugs into a standard outlet with the included charger. A full charge takes 8 to 10 hours — perfect overnight.

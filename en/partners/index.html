Frequently asked questions

Who can become a partner?

Vacation-rental agencies, concierges, property managers and villas that need golf carts on a recurring basis.

Does the program cost anything?

No. Becoming a partner is free; you only pay for rentals at your preferred rate, consolidated in your monthly account.

How do you invoice?

With a monthly e-CF invoice (electronic fiscal receipt), with no deposit per booking.

Ready to become a BOLT partner?

Message us and we'll set you up with your partner rate, no obligation.

FAQ — PCRC

Do you deliver inside Puntacana Resort & Club?

Yes, to villas and residences across the resort. We coordinate delivery with your host, concierge or property manager for access.

Can I drive anywhere in the resort?

The golf cart is for use inside residential areas, villas and permitted resort areas, following the resort's internal rules and BOLT's 25 km/h limit in residential zones.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend requesting availability as soon as you have your dates — in high season the fleet sells out. We confirm on WhatsApp as soon as possible.

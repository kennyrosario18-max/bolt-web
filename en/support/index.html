Support & manual

Operating, safety and charging your BOLT

Everything you need to drive your golf cart with confidence: start-up, brakes, battery, rules and emergencies — in one place.

Start the vehicle

  1. 01Lift the seat and set the main switch to RUN
  2. 02Set the gear lever to NEUTRAL
  3. 03Insert the key and turn to the on position
  4. 04Check the battery indicator before driving off
  5. 05Select F (forward) or R (reverse)
  6. 06Gently press the accelerator to begin

Turn off the vehicle

  1. 01Bring the cart to a complete stop
  2. 02Set the selector to NEUTRAL or parking
  3. 03Engage the parking brake
  4. 04Turn the key to OFF and remove it
  5. 05Set the switch under the seat to OFF

Pedals & gears

GO

Accelerator

Always on the right. Press gradually; avoid sudden acceleration and slow down in curves.

STOP

Brake

Always on the left. Brake smoothly and with anticipation; use the parking brake when stopped.

F·N·R

Gear selector

F forward, N neutral, R reverse (beeps when moving). ECO & Zycar: switch + key + F/R; Club Car: key + F/R.

Battery charging

Charging steps

  1. 01Verify the vehicle is fully OFF
  2. 02Connect the charger to the vehicle first
  3. 03Then plug the charger into the power outlet
  4. 04Verify the charging indicator is on
  5. 05Do not disconnect the charger while charging

8–10h

  • Full charge: 8–10 hours — perfect overnight.
  • Yellow indicator = charging · green = complete.
  • Do not move the vehicle while charging. Do not fully drain the battery.

✓ Allowed

  • Drivers 18+ with a valid license
  • Use only inside residential communities, villas and resorts
  • All passengers seated while moving
  • Respect capacity: 4 seats → 360 kg · 6 seats → 540 kg
  • Report failures or accidents immediately
  • Defensive driving and respect for internal rules

✕ Forbidden

  • ✕Driving under alcohol, drugs or impairing medication
  • ✕Minors or unlicensed drivers
  • ✕Driving on the beach, sand, public roads or unauthorized areas
  • ✕Overloading or standing passengers
  • ✕Reckless driving, stunts or commercial use
  • ✕Driving barefoot or in unsuitable footwear
